Welcome to our final project website: Advanced Cable Testing for ASML Lithography Machines. This work was conducted by Group 3 of the Mechanical Engineering Department at the Grove School of Engineering, City College of New York. The project was guided by Professor Ali Sadegh and sponsored by ASML, with supervision by Matthew Sorna, P.E.

This site presents our final report, broken down into web-friendly sections. Use the navigation bar above to explore the project’s problem statement, analysis, design development, experimental results, and more.
